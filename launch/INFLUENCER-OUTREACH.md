# Influencer Outreach Strategy - PosturePal Pro

**Goal:** Leverage influencer audiences for authentic app promotion, reviews, and user acquisition

**Budget Tiers:**
- $0: Barter (free Pro access only)
- $500: $200 for micro-influencer gifting/partnerships
- $1,000: $400-600 for paid partnerships
- $5,000: $1,000+ for mix of micro + mid-tier influencers

---

## 🎯 Target Influencer Profiles

### Tier 1: Micro-Influencers (5K-50K followers)

**Why Target:**
- Higher engagement rates (5-10% vs. 1-3% for larger accounts)
- More affordable / open to barter
- Authentic, trusted recommendations
- Niche audiences (better conversion)

**Target Niches:**

**1. Apple Watch Reviewers**
- Tech enthusiasts who review Apple Watch apps
- Unboxing/setup content creators
- Apple ecosystem fans

**Examples to Find:**
- Search Instagram: #AppleWatch #AppleWatchApps
- YouTube: "Apple Watch apps 2024"
- TikTok: #AppleWatchTips

**2. Health & Wellness Creators**
- Chiropractors with social media presence
- Physical therapists
- Posture/ergonomics specialists
- General wellness influencers

**3. Productivity/WFH Influencers**
- Remote work content creators
- Desk setup enthusiasts
- Productivity app reviewers
- Digital nomads

**4. Gaming/Esports Creators**
- Gamers who stream long sessions
- Esports health advocates
- Gaming setup reviewers

**5. Student Influencers**
- Study-with-me creators
- College productivity accounts
- Student life vloggers

---

### Tier 2: Mid-Tier Influencers (50K-200K followers)

**When to Approach:** After validating product-market fit with micros

**Target:**
- Established Apple Watch YouTubers
- Health/wellness Instagram accounts
- Productivity podcast hosts
- Tech review channels

**Cost Expectation:**
- Dedicated post: $200-500
- Story mention: $50-150
- Video review: $500-1,000
- Affiliate partnership: 20-30% commission

---

### Tier 3: Macro-Influencers (200K+ followers)

**Approach Strategy:** Unlikely to respond unless:
- You have traction (10K+ downloads)
- Affiliate deal with generous terms
- Unique angle (founder story, mission-driven)

**Long-term goal, not launch strategy**

## 📊 Influencer Evaluation Criteria

**Before Reaching Out, Check:**

1. **Engagement Rate**
   - Formula: (Likes + Comments) ÷ Followers × 100
   - Good: >3% for 10K-50K followers
   - Great: >5%

2. **Audience Fit**
   - Do their followers match your target? (desk workers, Apple Watch users, health-conscious)
   - Check comments: are they from your demographic?

3. **Content Quality**
   - Professional production (doesn't need to be perfect)
   - Consistent posting
   - Authentic voice (not overly scripted)

4. **Authenticity**
   - Do they only post ads? (Red flag)
   - Mix of organic + sponsored? (Good)
   - Honest reviews? (Best)

5. **Past Partnerships**
   - Have they promoted apps before?
   - How did they present them?
   - Comments section reaction?

## 🤝 Partnership Models

### Model 1: Free Product (Barter)
**Offer:** Free lifetime Pro access  
**Ask:** Honest review (no obligation to post)  
**Best for:** Micro-influencers, early validation  
**Conversion:** ~10% will post organically

### Model 2: Affiliate Only
**Offer:** 30% revenue share on all referred subscriptions  
**Ask:** Promotion with custom code  
**Best for:** Influencers confident in conversion  
**Pro:** Performance-based (no upfront cost)  
**Con:** Harder sell (no guaranteed payment)

### Model 3: Flat Fee + Post
**Offer:** $100-500 per post (based on reach)  
**Ask:** 1 dedicated post/video  
**Best for:** Mid-tier influencers  
**Pro:** Guaranteed content  
**Con:** Upfront cost, no performance guarantee

### Model 4: Hybrid (Flat Fee + Affiliate)
**Offer:** $200 for post + 20% ongoing commission  
**Ask:** Dedicated post + long-term partnership  
**Best for:** Best-fit influencers  
**Pro:** Upfront + long-term value

### Model 5: Product Gifting
**Offer:** App access + physical gift ($20-50 ergonomic product)  
**Ask:** Unboxing/review  
**Best for:** Lifestyle/unboxing creators  
**Pro:** More tangible, better content

## 📋 Outreach Workflow

### Phase 1: Research & List Building (Week -4)

**Goal:** 100 micro-influencers identified

**Process:**
1. Search platforms (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok)
2. Evaluate using criteria above
3. Add to spreadsheet:
   - Name
   - Platform
   - Handle
   - Follower count
   - Engagement rate
   - Email/DM contact
   - Notes (why they're a fit)

**Tool:** Google Sheet or Airtable

---

### Phase 2: Initial Outreach (Week -3 to -2)

**Goal:** Contact 50 influencers

**Process:**
1. Personalize email template (mention specific content of theirs)
2. Send email (or Instagram DM if no email found)
3. Log in spreadsheet:
   - Date contacted
   - Response (yes/no/pending)
   - Follow-up date

**Follow-up:** If no response in 5 days, send gentle follow-up

**Follow-up Template:**

Subject: Following up - PosturePal partnership

Hi [Name],

Just wanted to follow up on my email from [date] about PosturePal.

I know you're busy! If you're not interested, no worries at all. But if you'd like to try the app, just let me know and I'll send access right away.

Thanks!  
[Your Name]

---

### Phase 3: Onboarding & Delivery (Week -1 to Launch)

**When Influencer Says Yes:**

1. **Send Welcome Email:**
   - TestFlight link (or promo code if live)
   - Quick start guide
   - Key talking points (not a script)
   - Timeline expectations
   - Contact info for questions

2. **Provide Assets:**
   - App icon (high-res)
   - Screenshots
   - Demo video
   - Brand guidelines (optional)

3. **Talking Points (Not a Script):**
   - What problem PosturePal solves
   - How it works (gentle reminders)
   - Who it's for (desk workers, gamers, etc.)
   - Key features (especially Pro)
   - Your story (why you built it)

4. **Set Expectations:**
   - Post by [date] if possible
   - Honest review > fake hype
   - Tag @PosturePalApp
   - Use #PosturePal (optional)
   - Include App Store link or promo code

---

### Phase 4: Content Review & Amplification (Launch Week)

**When Influencer Posts:**

1. **Thank Them:**
   - Comment on their post
   - Share to your Stories (tag them)
   - Send thank-you DM/email

2. **Amplify:**
   - Repost to PosturePal channels
   - Include in newsletter
   - Screenshot for testimonials

3. **Track Performance:**
   - Did they use promo code? (track conversions)
   - Engagement on their post?
   - Traffic spike from their post?

4. **Nurture Relationship:**
   - Keep them updated on milestones
   - Send updates when new features launch
   - Consider long-term ambassador program

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Don't:**
1. **Mass DM without personalization** - gets ignored
2. **Demand specific messaging** - kills authenticity
3. **Only target mega-influencers** - waste of time at launch
4. **Ignore micro-influencers** - they often convert better
5. **Forget to follow up** - people are busy
6. **Overpromise** - be realistic about what your app does
7. **Neglect relationship after post** - one-and-done is shortsighted

**Do:**
1. **Personalize every outreach**
2. **Make it easy for them** (provide assets, clear info)
3. **Value their time**
4. **Be authentic** (they can smell bullshit)
5. **Follow up (once)**
6. **Track everything**
7. **Build long-term relationships**

## 🚀 Long-Term Influencer Strategy

**Month 1-3: Validation**
- Focus on micro-influencers
- Barter model (low cost)
- Learn what messaging works
- Build case studies

**Month 4-6: Scaling**
- Invest in mid-tier influencers (if budget + data support it)
- Launch affiliate program publicly
- Create ambassador tier (top performers get perks)

**Month 7-12: Ambassador Program**
- Formalize program
- Tiered benefits (based on referrals)
- Exclusive access to new features
- Revenue share increases with performance
- Community of brand advocates
