## 17. RED FLAGS TO AVOID

### ❌ DON'T:
- **Keyword stuff** the title (e.g., "PosturePal: Posture Reminder Tracker Back Pain Neck Relief") — looks spammy, Apple may reject
- **Repeat keywords** across title, subtitle, and keyword field (wastes space)
- **Use competitor brand names** in keyword field (against Apple guidelines)
- **Change title/subtitle frequently** (hurts rankings — pick and stick for 3+ months)
- **Ignore negative reviews** (respond quickly to prevent ranking drops)
- **Buy fake reviews/downloads** (Apple detects and bans)

### ✅ DO:
- **Keep it natural** — write for humans, optimize for robots second
- **Test and iterate** — ASO is not "set it and forget it"
- **Monitor constantly** — rankings change weekly
- **Respond to reviews** — builds trust AND boosts ASO
- **Update regularly** — "What's New" resets ranking algo
