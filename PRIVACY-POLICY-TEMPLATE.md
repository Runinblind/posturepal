## Introduction

PosturePal Pro ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the PosturePal Pro mobile application (the "App").

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address (for subscription management via RevenueCat)
- **Profile Data:** Name, posture goals (stored locally on your device)
- **Health Data:** Posture check-in timestamps, streak data (stored locally, optionally synced to HealthKit with your permission)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** App feature usage, screen views, button taps (anonymous analytics)
- **Device Information:** Device model, iOS version, App version
- **Crash Reports:** Anonymous crash logs to improve app stability

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ We do NOT sell your data to third parties
- ❌ We do NOT track your location
- ❌ We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other apps
- ❌ We do NOT share your health data with anyone

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
1. **Provide the Service:** Send posture reminders, track your progress, display analytics
2. **Process Subscriptions:** Manage your Pro subscription via RevenueCat
3. **Improve the App:** Anonymous usage analytics to identify bugs and improve features
4. **Customer Support:** Respond to your questions and technical issues
5. **App Store Reviews:** Track app installs and ratings (anonymous)

---

## Data Storage & Security

### Local Storage (On Your Device)
- All posture check-in data, streaks, and settings are stored **locally on your iPhone/Apple Watch**
- Data syncs between iPhone and Watch via Apple's WatchConnectivity (encrypted by iOS)
- Widget data is stored in App Groups (secure iOS storage)

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
- You can optionally enable iCloud backup for your data
- Data is encrypted by Apple and synced across your devices
- We do NOT have access to your iCloud data

### Third-Party Services
- **RevenueCat:** Subscription management (Email, subscription status, purchase history)
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- **Apple HealthKit (Optional):** Posture check-ins can be saved to HealthKit with your permission
  - Data is stored locally, controlled by Apple Health privacy settings
- **Apple Analytics:** Anonymous crash reports and usage statistics
  - Controlled by iOS "Share Analytics" setting

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all data stored in the app (Settings → Export Data)
- **Delete:** Clear all your data (Settings → Reset All Data)
- **Export:** Export your check-in history as CSV (Settings → Export Data)
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics (iOS Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements)
- **Cancel Subscription:** Managed via Apple App Store subscriptions

---

## Data Retention

- **Active Users:** Data retained while you use the app
- **Subscription Data:** Retained by RevenueCat for billing purposes (up to 7 years per tax law)
- **Deleted Accounts:** All local data is immediately deleted when you uninstall the app or reset data
