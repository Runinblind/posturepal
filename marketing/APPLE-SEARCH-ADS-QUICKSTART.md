**Goal:** Drive high-intent downloads at $5-10 CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)

---

## 🎯 Why Apple Search Ads?

- **High Intent:** Users searching App Store are ready to download
- **Controlled Spend:** Set daily budget as low as $10/day
- **Fast Setup:** Campaign live in 15 minutes
- **Better Than Alternatives:** Lower CPA than Facebook/Instagram for app downloads

**Expected Results (First 30 Days):**
- Budget: $300-900 ($10-30/day)
- Downloads: 60-180
- CPA: $5-10 (industry average for health apps)
- Conversion to Pro: 3-5% of downloads

## 🎨 Creative Best Practices

Apple Search Ads pulls from your App Store listing, so optimize:

### App Icon
- **Clear:** Visible at thumbnail size (40x40px)
- **On-Brand:** Matches PosturePal's vibe (health + tech)
- **No Text:** Icons with text perform worse

### Screenshots (First 3 are CRITICAL)
1. **Screenshot 1:** Hero shot - App in use with clear benefit
   - Bad: "Welcome to PosturePal"
   - Good: "Fix Your Posture in 30 Days" + before/after visual

2. **Screenshot 2:** Key feature - Smart reminders
   - Show notification: "Time for a posture check! 💪"

3. **Screenshot 3:** Tracking/Progress
   - Show streak counter, progress graph

### App Preview Video (Optional but 30% boost)
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds = problem ("Does your back hurt?")
- **Solution:** Next 10 seconds = app demo
- **CTA:** Last 5 seconds = "Download now"

### App Title & Subtitle
- **Title:** "PosturePal Pro" (brand consistency)
- **Subtitle:** "Posture Reminder & Tracker" (keyword-rich, under 30 chars)

### Description (First 170 Characters Matter Most)
"Fix your desk posture with smart reminders. Track progress, build streaks, and eliminate back pain. The Duolingo for posture. Free with Pro features."

## 🎯 Target CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) Goals

### Week 1: Discovery Phase
- **Target CPA:** Don't stress it yet
- **Focus:** Gather data on which keywords convert
- **Action:** Let campaigns run, monitor daily

### Week 2-4: Optimization Phase
- **Target CPA:** $7-10
- **Focus:** Pause underperforming keywords
- **Action:** Increase bids on keywords with CPA <$7

### Month 2+: Scaling Phase
- **Target CPA:** $5-8
- **Focus:** Scale winning keywords
- **Action:** Increase budgets on campaigns with CPA <$5

**Formula:**
```
CPA = Total Spend ÷ Total Downloads
```

**Example:**
- Spend: $300
- Downloads: 50
- CPA: $6 ✅ (Good!)

**What's a "Good" CPA?**
- Under $5: Excellent (scale aggressively)
- $5-10: Good (maintain/optimize)
- $10-15: Acceptable (test improvements)
- Over $15: Too high (pause campaign)

- [ ] Identify top 3 performing keywords
- [ ] Pause bottom 30% of keywords

## 🚀 Campaign Setup (Step-by-Step)

### Before You Start
- [ ] App must be live on App Store (not just TestFlight)
- [ ] Payment method added to Apple Search Ads account
- [ ] App Store listing optimized (screenshots, description)

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to searchads.apple.com
2. Sign in with Apple ID (same as App Store Connect)
3. Link PosturePal Pro from your apps list

### Step 2: Create Campaign 1 (Brand)
1. Click "+ Campaign"
2. Name: "PosturePal - Brand"
3. Countries: Start with US only (expand later)
4. Budget: $5/day
5. CPA Goal: Leave blank for now
6. Ad Group: "Brand Keywords"
7. Add keywords manually:
   - posturepal
   - posture pal
   - posturepal pro
8. Max CPT Bid: $2.00
9. Audience: All users
10. Creative: Use default App Store assets
11. Launch!

### Step 3: Create Campaign 2 (Intent)
1. Repeat steps above
2. Name: "PosturePal - Intent"
3. Budget: $15/day
4. Ad Group: "High Intent"
5. Add keywords from Tier 1 & 2 (Keywords 1-10)

2. Go to Settings → Attribution

   - Redownloads
   - Pro subscription starts (if trackable)

## 🛠️ Optimization Tactics

### Tactic 1: The "Pause & Pivot"
- After 7 days, pause any keyword with:
  - 100+ impressions, 0 downloads
  - CPA >$15
- Reallocate budget to winning keywords

### Tactic 2: The "Bid Ladder"
- Start bids at $1.00
- If no impressions after 2 days, increase by $0.25
- Stop at $2.00 (unless CPA is still profitable)

### Tactic 3: The "Negative Keyword List"
Exclude these searches (they won't convert):
- "free posture app" (price-sensitive users)
- "posture brace" (looking for physical products, not apps)
- "chiropractor" (seeking professional help)
- "games" (wrong audience)

### Tactic 4: The "Dayparting" (Advanced)
- Apple Search Ads doesn't support scheduling
- BUT you can manually pause campaigns during low-conversion hours
- Check Analytics: Do most downloads happen 9 AM-5 PM? Focus there.

### Tactic 5: The "Competitor Keyword Test" (Risky)
- Bid on competitor app names (e.g., "upright go", "posture pal reminder")
- Apple allows this, but competitors may bid on yours too
- Only do this if your App Store listing is STRONGER than theirs

### When to Scale (After 30 Days)
If you've achieved:
- CPA consistently <$8

**Then:**

If after 14 days:
- CPA >$15

**Then:**
- Pause campaigns

- Relaunch in 2 weeks

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Setting budget too low** - Under $10/day won't get enough data
2. **Bidding too high out the gate** - Start conservative, scale up
3. **Ignoring Search Match terms** - Review weekly; they auto-add keywords
4. **Not using negative keywords** - You'll waste money on irrelevant searches
5. **Giving up after Week 1** - Optimization takes 2-3 weeks minimum
6. **Focusing on installs, not conversions** - 1,000 downloads at $20 CPA is worse than 100 at $5 CPA

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Start small, scale fast** - Better to test $10/day for a week than blow $300 in 3 days
2. **Screenshots matter more than keywords** - 80% of conversion happens at the listing
3. **Monitor daily, optimize weekly** - Don't panic-adjust after 1 bad day

## 🚦 When to Stop ASA

If after 60 days:
- CPA is still >$12
- LTV (Lifetime Value) of users <$10
- Organic growth is outpacing paid

**Then:** Pause ASA and focus on organic channels (Product Hunt, Reddit, content marketing).

**Apple Search Ads works best when:**
- You have a strong App Store listing
- Your app has proven product-market fit
- You can afford to lose money upfront for long-term LTV
